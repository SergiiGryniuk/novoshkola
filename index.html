Voices of Our
Families

Not long ago I was a student at Novopecherska School and, in fact, one of those who joined the School when it first warmly opened its doors. Thanks to the knowledge and experience I gained here, I was able to enrol in two of Canada's top universities: the University of Toronto and The University of British Columbia.

H
Hryhorii Ovcharenko
Class of 2021/2022 · University of Toronto

Novopecherska School taught me that the community you spend your school years with plays a crucial role. I chose a faculty at KPI, where students with the highest national exam scores enrol. My coursemates are remarkably smart, ambitious and proactive — in this atmosphere you want to keep growing constantly, just like at Novopecherska.

K
Kyrylo Hilovyants
Class of 2019/2020 · KPI

I am grateful to the School for its enduring values — values that live not on paper but in the hearts and minds of a truly professional team. The School walks alongside the family, and at times gently guides it, nudging everyone upward and forward together. Four of our children already attend the School and the preschool, and I cannot recall a single day when they weren't eager to rush in.

O
Oksana Samusieva
Mother of primary and middle school students

Novopecherska School is the kind of nurturing space for personal growth that we, as adults, so often missed in our own school years. The School is not only solid academic knowledge but also growth, mutual understanding, and genuine friendship between students and teachers. Here every pupil is a leader and an innovator, encouraged to pursue their own dreams.
